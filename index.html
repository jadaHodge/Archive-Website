About Us

Archive is a kindred dance collective that features individuals coming from different backgrounds, each of whom offers different perspectives in a kinetic collaboration. Creative input of each dancer through freestyle and choreography birthed a piece that is truly an "archive" of movement, which shows that 22 heads working together is better than one. Archive's mission is to cultivate authenticity in dancers and move the audience into experiences that create a visceral response that stem from relatable scenarios.

Words from the Director

I brought Archive Dance Collective together by selecting movers I have known throughout my dance journey. As a tarot card reader, I was initially inspired by the Page of Cups to create something new and different. Something that would challenge the social norms.
