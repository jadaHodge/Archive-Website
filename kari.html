BIO

Karina Cervantes is a movement aficionado, performer, choreographer, and is interested in researching movement as energy, energy deriving of the sound waves created by music and sound. The investigation started at a young age as a dance student with Arte Flamenco de San José. She then pursued the art form at San José State University graduating with a Bachelor of Art’s in Dance. Upon graduating, Karina founded Cypher Dance Company, a multidisciplinary dance company aimed to make dance accessible for all dancers and audience members. To further her training and movement research Karina has trained in various dance genres all over the Bay Area including flamenco, ballet, contemporary, modern and urban street styles.

Her choreographic works have appeared at DeAnza College, MACLA, KIPP Navigate Public Charter, LEVYsalon, War Memorial Veterans building, SUBZERO, Christmas in the Park, The San Jose Museum of Art and the Mexican Heritage Plaza during the Ultima Parada. Karina has performed with groups including Archive Dance Collective, The LAB (freestyle group, Hayward CA), Līv Dance Collective and Cypher Dance Compnany.

Currently, Karina is the director or Cypher Dance Company, a musician for Corazón Salvaje, a dancer on Archive Dance Collective and a dance instructor at On One Studios.
