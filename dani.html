BIO

Danielle Galvez from Union City, CA. She is a dancer, choreographer, performer, and movement researcher that investigates human emotions through kinesthetic response to sound (a fancy way to say freestyle).

She first started her dance journey doing color guard. After realizing her passion in movement, she pursued Dance at San Jose State University. In her pursuit in understanding the different dialects of dance, Danielle finds herself in many different (at times polarizing) dance environments that have attributed her with an eclectic style. She currently is a company dancer of Cypher Dance Company.

As a yoga instructor, she integrates yogic philosophy in her dance endeavors. Danielle is the director and founder of Archive Dance Collective.
