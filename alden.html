BIO

Alden V. Apusen is an aspiring dancer, writer, and Super Smash Bros player. He got his start in dance pretty late compared to most at the age of 16 when he joined his high school's urban choreography team.

From there he went on to perform for other competitive dance teams such as Rhythm Inc., Hungry Bumz, Syndicate, and as of current day Archive Dance Co.

Nowadays, Alden finds himself immersed in street styles such as Popping, Hip-Hop, and Krump in order to further understand where urban choreography gets its movement from.

Unrelated to dance, he got his BA in English at Cal State East Bay and is striving to be the best Smash Ultimate/Melee player in his region.
